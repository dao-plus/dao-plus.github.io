About

+ Everything You Need To Know.





Our goal is to provide a platform for individuals and organizations to come together and share ideas, resources, and expertise in a transparent and decentralized manner. By leveraging the power of Web3 technologies such as blockchain, we aim to create a self-sustaining ecosystem that empowers individuals to drive innovation and make a positive impact in the world.

The core foundation of DAO+ is established on a vibrant and varied assembly of people, all of whom possess a shared enthusiasm for blockchain technology and decentralized governance. This community's members enjoy exclusive access to events, workshops, and discussion platforms, enabling them to network with those who share their interests and cultivate meaningful connections within the ecosystem.

To incentivize community participation, DAO+ has implemented a unique reward system based on Soulbond Token (SBT) and Non-Fungible Token (NFT). Members can earn SBT and NFT by contributing to the DAO+ community or attending DAO+ events and participating in community activities. These tokens serve as a form of recognition for community participation, as well as a means to access exclusive resources and benefits within the DAO+ ecosystem.

Our various NFT and SBT are the access to our community as well as identity levels of members is partly defined and distinguished by the points recorded by the SBT. They can also be used to access exclusive resources and benefits within the DAO+ ecosystem.

The financial backbone of DAO+ aims to support innovative projects and ventures within the blockchain and broader technology ecosystems. The DAO+ fund is structured as a venture capital (VC) and private equity (PE) fund, which raises money from limited partners (LPs) who share a vision for supporting innovative and impactful projects across different industries and technologies, operating under decentralized management through the community.

DAO+ Accelerator is dedicated to supporting and accelerating innovative blockchain-based projects. As a vital part of our ecosystem, the accelerator works closely with the DAO+ Fund, which screens and selects the most promising projects from the proposal pool. At DAO+, we believe that our culture and values are the vital elements contributing to our accomplishments. We strive to cultivate a community that embraces love, transparency, collaboration, inclusivity, and social impact as fundamental principles. Our commitment to these values is reflected in everything we do, from the way we communicate and make decisions to the projects we support and the impact we have on the wider world.
